About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Chen, a final-year Electrical Engineering student at Massachusetts Institute of Technology with a passion for embedded systems and IoT solutions. My journey in engineering began when I disassembled my first electronic toy at age 10, curious about what made it work.

Throughout my academic career, I've focused on bridging the gap between hardware and software, developing skills in microcontroller programming, PCB design, and system integration. I believe that the most innovative solutions come from understanding both the electrical components and the software that drives them.

Outside of academics, I enjoy participating in hackathons, contributing to open-source projects, and mentoring junior engineering students. I'm currently seeking opportunities in embedded systems development where I can apply my technical knowledge to create impactful solutions.
